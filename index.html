ISO Standards

creating new opportunities

ISO ensure products and services are safe, reliable and of excellent quality. For business, they are strategic tools that scale back costs by minimizing waste and increasing productivity.

They assist firms to access new markets, level the field for developing countries and facilitate free and fair trade.

Accreditation

helping companies gain recognition

Accreditation provides strategic tools and guidelines to assist corporations tackle a number of the foremost exigent challenges of contemporary business.

They make sure that business operations are as efficient as possible, increase productivity and facilitate access to new markets.

Benefits

Worldwide Access

opening international gateways to business

The existence of various National or regional Standards can produce technical barriers to trade and increase the price of doing business.

International Standards offer the technical basis on which political Trade Agreements can be put into practice, at both regional or International level.
